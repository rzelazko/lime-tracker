Install App

To install this app on your iPhone or iPad:

  1. Tap the Share button ios_share in the Safari toolbar
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add" in the top right corner

info Note: This app must be installed from Safari. If you're using another browser, please open this page in Safari first.

To install this app:

  1. Click the menu icon (three dots) in the top right corner of your browser
  2. Select "Install Lime Tracker" or "Add to Home screen"
  3. Follow the prompts to complete installation

Your browser may support installing this app as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

Supported browsers:

  • Chrome (desktop and Android)
  • Edge (desktop and mobile)
  • Safari (iOS 11.3+)
  • Samsung Internet (Android)

lightbulb Tip: Look for an "Install" or "Add to Home Screen" option in your browser's menu.
